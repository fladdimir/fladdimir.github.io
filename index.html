    Wladimir Hofmann

    Software Developer, Simulation Engineer

    Biography

    Wladimir is a Software Engineer currently working in Hamburg with Lufthansa Industry Solutions, where he is part of an agile development team.
    Being born in Dresden and completing high-school in Berlin, he later lived in Magdeburg, Grenoble, and Tampere.
    While studying he specialized in logistics simulation technologies. Completed projects span various domains, ranging from urban transport optimization to digital twins of manufacturing systems.
    In his free time you may find him tinkering around with new technologies, doing calisthenics, or just spending time with his fiancée and friends in Hamburg-Altona.
